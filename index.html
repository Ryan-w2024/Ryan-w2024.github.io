PoseAnything: Universal Pose-guided Video Generation with Part-aware Temporal Coherence

Ruiyan Wang, Teng Hu, Kaihui Huang, Zihan Su, Ran Yi, Lizhuang Ma

Shanghai Jiao Tong University

arXiv GitHub
Abstract: Pose-guided video generation enables precise control over subject motion through pose sequences. Current methods are limited to human poses only, generalizing poorly to other subjects. We propose PoseAnything, the first universal pose-guided video generation framework supporting both human and non-human characters with arbitrary skeletal inputs.
